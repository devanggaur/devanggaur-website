Experience & Background

8+ years transforming payment systems at enterprise scale

Building the Future of Payments

As a Senior Product Manager with deep expertise in global payments and AI-powered optimization, I've spent nearly a decade transforming how enterprises handle payment processing, fraud detection, and risk management.

My journey spans leading product initiatives at Adobe, where I pioneered ML platforms and expanded payment operations across global markets, to PayPal, where I architected real-time fraud detection systems processing billions in annual volume, and Dell Technologies, where I revamped checkout experiences generating millions in additional revenue.

My expertise lies at the intersection of payments technology and artificial intelligence—building systems that don't just process transactions, but intelligently optimize every aspect of the payment lifecycle. From implementing PSD2 compliance and 3DS2 authentication across markets to designing ML-powered retry strategies and fraud detection engines, I focus on turning complex payment challenges into measurable business outcomes.
