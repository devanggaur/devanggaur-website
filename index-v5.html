About — Devang Gaur
Devang Gaur
Product Manager, Google Payments
Active — San Francisco Bay Area

At Google, I'm part of the Payments organization building the consumer platform powering YouTube, Google Play, and Google Store. My team owns the buyflow infrastructure — the critical path from "tap to pay" to completed transaction. We build Full Buyflow (hosted checkout with product details, payment selection, and legal messaging) and Lite Buyflow (lightweight embedded integration for product-controlled experiences).

Before Google, I drove payment innovation at Adobe — ML platforms, global expansion, $40M+ revenue impact — and at PayPal, building fraud detection systems processing billions in annual volume.
