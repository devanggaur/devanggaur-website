01 — About

I build payment products used by billions of people every day.

At Google, I'm part of the Payments organization building the consumer payments platform that powers first-party products like YouTube, Google Play, and Google Store.

My team owns payment buyflows and platform capabilities — the infrastructure that sits between a user tapping "Buy" and a transaction completing. That includes:

Full Buyflow Lite Buyflow Platform Capabilities

Full Buyflow is a hosted checkout that handles product details, payment method selection, legal messaging, and completion. Lite Buyflow is a lightweight embedded integration where the product team controls parts of the experience.

Before Google, I drove payment innovation at Adobe and PayPal — building fraud detection systems, ML-powered optimization platforms, and expanding payment operations across global markets.
