At Google, I'm part of the Payments organization building the consumer platform that powers YouTube, Google Play, and Google Store. My team owns the buyflow infrastructure — the critical path from "tap to pay" to completed transaction.

We build two core platform capabilities. Full Buyflow is a hosted checkout that handles product details, payment method selection, legal messaging, and completion. Lite Buyflow is a lightweight embedded integration where the product team controls parts of the experience.

Before Google, I drove payment innovation at Adobe — ML platforms, global expansion — and at PayPal, building fraud detection systems processing billions in annual volume.
