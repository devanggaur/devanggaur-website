Concept 4: "The Minimalist"

Brutalist, High-Impact Simplicity

Key Features:

  • Ultra-minimal, brutalist design
  • Black & white with single accent color
  • Strong typography hierarchy
  • Maximum whitespace
  • Direct, no-nonsense presentation
  • Focus on credibility & authority
Best For: Establishing maximum authority and professionalism - letting your credentials and content speak for themselves

Strengths

  • Maximum authority
  • Timeless design
  • Fast loading

Considerations

  • May feel stark
  • Less personality
  • Could seem austere

My Recommendation: Concept 1 + 2 Hybrid

Based on your content and goals, I'd recommend a hybrid approach combining:

  • From Concept 1: Modern, technical aesthetic with subtle animations to show payments expertise
  • From Concept 2: Content-first layout to showcase your thought leadership articles
  • Color scheme: Professional dark blues/teals (payment industry colors) with white/light sections
  • Structure:
    • Hero: Bold introduction with animated payment flow element
    • Featured: Latest articles/media coverage in grid
    • Expertise: Two columns - Payments | AI Product Management
    • Video Series: Embedded LinkedIn content
    • Publications: Full article listing
    • Contact: Simple, direct CTA

This approach: Establishes technical credibility while keeping your thought leadership front and center. Modern enough to show you understand current tech, professional enough for enterprise audiences.
